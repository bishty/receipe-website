Kale Caesar Quinoa salad with Roasted Bread

Kale and quinoa provide a healthy base for roasted bread topped with a light Caesar sauce.

Total time:45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Kale
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Bread
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparaton

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast bread until golden brown and 165 in middle

    Time:40 minutes

  2. Toss quinoa,bread,kale, and Caesar dressing until coated

    Time:40 minutes

  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as garnish.

    Time:40 minutes
